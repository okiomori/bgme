# Character Design Brief

## Steam intent

- Characters must read at `1920x1080` screenshots and in reduced capsule crops.
- Each silhouette needs one dominant shape visible in fog: halo, hammer block, moth-wing, map-ribbon.
- Avoid over-detail on costumes; contrast is carried by large value groups first, ornaments second.

## Party heroes

### Лиора, Проводница мха

- Role: support / control
- Silhouette: slim vertical body, long luminous hair, circular halo-sigil behind the back
- Materials: parchment cloth, dew enamel, living moss, brushed gold trim
- Steam pose: three-quarter view, one palm forward with a lumen orb

### Риан, Камнерез святилищ

- Role: vanguard / breaker
- Silhouette: broad chest, short neck, rectangular hammer, bark mantle
- Materials: basalt, bronze rivets, bark, lichen, muted steel
- Steam pose: weight on one leg, hammer on shoulder, torso turned to camera

### Сайя, Охотница красной моли

- Role: hunter / debuff
- Silhouette: narrow body, asymmetrical moth-wing cloak, long fan-blade
- Materials: burnt silk, feathered moth wing, bone inserts, lacquered dark leather
- Steam pose: side profile with one wing opened into the frame

### Ноэль, Архивистка дождевых карт

- Role: control / explorer
- Silhouette: medium body, hanging ribbon-maps, diagonal staff
- Materials: paper layers, steel compass fittings, watercolor inks, rain-glass beads
- Steam pose: map opened in one hand, staff cutting a diagonal line through the frame

## Boss

### Немый Колосс

- Role: chapter boss
- Silhouette: moss-covered stone knight, broken sword, chapel-scale torso, butterflies as contrast read
- Materials: wet stone, cracked runes, thick moss, pale cyan lumen seams
- Steam pose: low camera, sword vertical, one shoulder collapsed and overgrown

## Guardrails

- No glossy sci-fi metal
- No neon purple
- No small noisy accessories that disappear in Steam crops
- Keep faces simple and readable before adding costume detail
